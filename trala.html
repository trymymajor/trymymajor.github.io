About T-Kam Essential Oil

T-Kam Essential Oil is a premium product born from the rich biodiversity of East Kalimantan, Indonesia. Our essential oil is extracted through a meticulous cold-press process from the indigenous Tiwai onion, known for its exceptional therapeutic properties.

We collaborate directly with local farmers in Kalimantan, ensuring ethical sourcing while supporting sustainable agricultural practices. Our production process preserves 100% of the beneficial compounds, delivering nature's purest form of wellness.

Every bottle of T-Kam Essential Oil represents our commitment to quality, purity, and the preservation of Kalimantan's unique biodiversity.

Usage Guide

Aromatherapy: Add 3-5 drops to your diffuser with water. Run for 30-60 minutes in a well-ventilated area.

Topical Use: Dilute 1-2 drops with a carrier oil (like coconut or jojoba oil) before applying to skin.

Massage: Mix 5-10 drops with 30ml carrier oil for a relaxing therapeutic massage.

Storage Tips

Store in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight. Keep the bottle tightly closed when not in use.

For best quality, use within 12 months of opening. The shelf life is typically 2-3 years when stored properly.
